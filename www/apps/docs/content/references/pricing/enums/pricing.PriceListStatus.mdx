# PriceListStatus

The price list's status.

## ACTIVE

 **ACTIVE** = `"active"`

The price list is enabled and its prices can be used.

___

## DRAFT

 **DRAFT** = `"draft"`

The price list is disabled, meaning its prices can't be used yet.

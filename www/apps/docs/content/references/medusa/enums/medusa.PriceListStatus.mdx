# PriceListStatus

The status of a price list.

## Enumeration Members

### ACTIVE

 **ACTIVE** = `"active"`

The price list is active, meaning its prices are applied to customers.

___

### DRAFT

 **DRAFT** = `"draft"`

The price list is a draft, meaning its not yet applied to customers.
